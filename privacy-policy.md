# Privacy Policy for GroceryGenius

**Effective Date:** February 10, 2026

GroceryGenius respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect
We collect the following information to provide core app functionality:
- Email address (for account creation and authentication)
- User ID (for account identification)

## Microphone Usage
GroceryGenius may request access to the microphone to support voice input features.
Audio is processed in real time on the device and is not stored or uploaded to any server.

## How We Use Information
Collected information is used only to:
- Authenticate users
- Provide app functionality
- Maintain account security

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for advertising or tracking purposes.

## Data Security
We use reasonable security measures to protect user data.
